Why join WCI art?

Our productions are selected with our students in mind and offer a variety of various opportunities through their high school year. In the past, we've participated in the Sears Drama Festival, collaborated with our incredible orchestra for large-scale musicals, explored Shakespeare, and each year we offer a student run drama festival in the spring.
